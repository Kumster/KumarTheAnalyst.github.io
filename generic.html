Business Insights 360

Business Insights 360 is a comprehensive Power BI project designed to deliver actionable insights across sales, marketing, finance, supply chain, and customer support operations.

Built a multi-page dashboard using Power BI to analyze Net Sales, Gross Margin, Forecast Accuracy, Risk Profiles, and more. The dashboards cater to stakeholders ranging from marketing teams to executive leadership, providing an intuitive UI and data-driven storytelling with advanced DAX measures.

Due to Power BI licensing restrictions, the interactive report cannot be embedded live. However, the Power BI file, project screenshots, and a detailed PDF walkthrough are available for reference on GitHub and as a downloadable document.
